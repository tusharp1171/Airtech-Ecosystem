Manufacturing Capabilities

Our modern Manufacturing Plant is located at Greater Pune, Maharashtra, India, and is equipped with advanced technology. We have a high-pressure foaming line with a production capacity of 4.50 lakhs sq.m per annum of PUF panels from the discontinuous line and another 5 lakhs sq.m per annum from the continuous line.

These capabilities enable us to execute turnkey projects of all sizes, including Prefabricated buildings, Houses, Cold Storage, Clean Rooms, Factories, and more. Our technology and capacity make us a reliable partner in the execution of large-scale projects.
